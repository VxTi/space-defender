How to play
Hello beginning user!
Welcome to Defender!
Before pressing Play, one must first know the controls.
First, get on thy spaceship. Once aboard, one can move the ship by either occupying the wireless or wired controller
If you're using the wireless one, the controls are as followed:
To move the spaceship, use the D-Pad buttons (←↑→↓) or the W, A, S and D keys. To shoot, press the A or SPACEbutton.
To pause the game, press the middle button or the R key.
To release an explosion, press B or the F key
